VISUAL STYLE
Cinematic dark romance set inside a sprawling Chicago mansion — marble countertops, warm amber light, deep bordeaux walls and rich dark wood. Palette of ivory, charcoal, deep black and gold. Mood: tension wrapped in elegance, the quiet danger of a house that was never meant to be gentle. Close-up framing, soft dramatic backlighting that leaves half a face in shadow.

[Character 1: DANTE MORETTI] — the mafia boss, most feared man in Chicago
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: Strong jaw with faint stubble, thick dark brown hair swept back, dark piercing eyes, olive-tan skin, visible tattoos covering the neck and both arms (sleeve tattoos reaching the hands)
- Age/Build: Mid-to-late 30s, tall and powerfully built — broad shoulders, defined arms, imposing presence that fills a doorway
- Signature wardrobe: Black dress shirt (unbuttoned at collar), dark tailored trousers; also seen in full black tuxedo with bow tie for formal events; gold matte signet ring on the right hand with the letter M engraved
- Ref image: thumb_ref.png (the man)

[Character 2: MINA BELLUCCI] — the maid, the narrator, quiet and resilient
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: Dark curly/wavy hair worn up in a loose bun on duty, let down at night; dark expressive eyes, smooth olive-tan skin, naturally beautiful without makeup, no visible jewelry
- Age/Build: Mid-to-late 20s, slender and graceful — the kind of build that moves silently through corridors
- Signature wardrobe: White linen button-up blouse tied at the waist, dark trousers or simple skirt, small apron on duty; off-duty: a dark navy blue dress (her mother's, simple round neckline) and a small worn fabric clutch bag
- Ref image: thumb_ref.png (the woman)
