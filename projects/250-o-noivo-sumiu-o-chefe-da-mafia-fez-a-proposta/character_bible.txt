VISUAL STYLE
Cinematic elegance with a dark-romance undertone. Grand interiors — marble, dark wood, crystal chandeliers, tall windows flooded with diffuse natural light and warm candlelight. Color palette: ivory, cream, deep navy, charcoal black, and gold accents. The mood is opulent and tension-filled: the world of old money, power kept quiet, and desire carefully controlled.

[Character 1: LUDOVICO CARACCIOLO] — the mafia don, feared protector, the hero
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: Distinguished silver-gray hair, combed back with precision; short, well-kept stubble along a strong jaw; dark, intense eyes that hold full attention — the kind that already know you before you speak; light tan complexion, a faint scar at the left temple
- Age/Build: Mid-50s. Tall, broad-shouldered, commanding — a frame that fills a doorway without effort
- Signature wardrobe: Dark navy fitted suit, crisp white shirt, black tie, white pocket square; in private moments — white shirt open at the collar, sleeves rolled to the elbow, old watch with a worn leather strap on the left wrist

[Character 2: IOLANDA MARSICANO] — the jilted bride, the narrator, the heroine
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: Dark chestnut brown hair worn in an elegant updo (low chignon with loose face-framing strands); warm olive skin; soft dark eyes; delicate features with quiet strength beneath them; small pearl or diamond drop earrings
- Age/Build: Late 20s. Slender, graceful posture; a woman who learned to hold herself upright under pressure
- Signature wardrobe: White off-the-shoulder bridal gown with lace bodice and flowing skirt, white veil (intro/opening scenes); dark navy midi dress with thin straps (public dinner scenes); light robe, hair loose (private mansion scenes)
