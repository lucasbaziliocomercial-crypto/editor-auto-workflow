VISUAL STYLE
Cinematic and opulent — warm amber and gold lighting against deep shadow, evoking a high-end New Orleans restaurant at night. City skyline glows through floor-to-ceiling windows behind the couple. The palette is rich: champagne, black, ivory, candlelight orange. Mood is tense and romantic, danger underneath the elegance.

- Age/Build: Late 20s; tall and broad-shouldered, built with controlled power — not bulky, but unmistakably commanding
- Signature wardrobe: Classic black tuxedo with a crisp white dress shirt, black tie, white pocket square; Italian-cut, impeccably fitted
