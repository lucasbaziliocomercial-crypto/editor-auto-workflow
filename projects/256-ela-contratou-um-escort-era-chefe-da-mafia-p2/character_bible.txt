VISUAL STYLE
Cinematic dark romance. The palette is deep gold, amber, and near-black — penthouse glass walls, city skyline at night, warm lamp glow cutting through shadows. Lighting is low and directional, sculpting faces with hard contrast. The mood is dangerous intimacy: luxury settings that hide lethal secrets.

[Character 1: EVREN MALVANI] — the mafia boss hero
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: strong jaw with a clean, close-shaved beard; dark brown hair combed back with natural flow, no product stiffness; intense dark eyes that scan a room before landing on a person; olive-tan skin with visible neck tattoos crawling from the collar
- Age/Build: early-to-mid 30s; tall, broad-shouldered, lean-muscular — fills a tailored jacket without effort
- Signature wardrobe: fitted white dress shirt open at the top two buttons, dress pants; in formal scenes, matte black tuxedo tailored to his exact frame
- Ref image: thumb_ref.png (male figure, right side)

[Character 2: NOELIA CROSS] — the hotel events manager heroine
- Face/Hair/Eyes/Skin: long, thick wavy dark brown hair falling past the shoulders; full lips, dark expressive eyes; olive-tan skin, naturally luminous; refined features with a grounded, composed expression
- Age/Build: late 20s to early 30s; lean, feminine silhouette — poised posture, the bearing of someone used to running a room
- Signature wardrobe: gold/champagne satin slip dress, deep V-neckline, mid-thigh length; bare shoulders; in later scenes, a white hotel robe with embroidered initials on the sleeve
- Ref image: thumb_ref.png (female figure, left side)
